Project 002 — Utility Tool — 2025

Outfit Colour
Palette
Analyser

Enter up to 5 clothing colours and receive a harmony analysis. Tells you whether your outfit palette is complementary, analogous, or triadic—and scores it.

Honest note: I built this while reading about colour theory for the first time. I wanted to understand why certain outfit combinations feel right. The scoring is simplified—not scientifically precise—but it taught me a lot about HSL colour spaces.
Year
2025
Stack
Vanilla JavaScript · HSL calculations · CSS
What I learned
Colour theory, HSL vs RGB, how designers think about palette building
