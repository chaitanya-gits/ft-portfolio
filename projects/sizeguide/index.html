Size Guide

Size conversion tool for clothing and shoes between Indian and Italian standards. Covers women's tops, bottoms, shoes, and men's shirts and trousers.

Honest note: I built this to solve a real frustration. When I started researching Italian brands and trying to understand sizing in context, there was no clean, reliable tool. I gathered the data from manufacturer sizing charts and built this. It is simple, but I use it.

Important: Sizing varies by brand and cut. Italian luxury brands (Prada, Gucci, Bottega Veneta) and fast fashion (Zara Italy) may have different sizing practices within the same system.
